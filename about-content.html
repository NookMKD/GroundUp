ABOUT

Welcome to "GROUND UP: SOCIAL ENTREPRENEURIAL ECOSYSTEM BOOST FOR RESILIENT CITIES." We're thrilled to share that our journey that kicked off in November 2021.


This dynamic initiative, supported by the Erasmus + program, is all about cultivating innovative methodologies that empower youth organizations and changemakers to cultivate vibrant ecosystems that amplify social entrepreneurial endeavors. Our diverse consortium comprises 11 dedicated organizations spanning the globe, including North Macedonia, Costa Rica, South Africa, Romania, Bulgaria, Slovenia, Austria, Denmark, Ecuador, Serbia, and Peru.



At the heart of "GROUND UP: SOCIAL ENTREPRENEURIAL ECOSYSTEM BOOST FOR RESILIENT CITIES" lies a visionary approach. We're crafting a place-based methodology that zeroes in on youth-centric social entrepreneurial activity, all within the unique tapestry of cities and local communities.



The mission of the project is to :

- Illuminate the challenges and hurdles that impede nurturing environments.

- Fortify social entrepreneurial ecosystems from their very foundations, enabling cities to forge ingenious avenues for empowering young trailblazers.

- Extract invaluable insights from impactful initiatives and projects, fostering a ripple effect of positive social and environmental change.

- Delve into the aspirations and motivations of stakeholders committed to fostering social entrepreneurial ecosystems.

- Synthesize a cohesive, evidence-driven framework spotlighting the pivotal elements that spell success at the local level.

Join us on this transformative journey as we collectively lay the groundwork for resilient cities that flourish through empowered youth-driven innovation. Together, we're shaping a future where social entrepreneurship thrives and communities prosper.
