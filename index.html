• When a group of young people from 11 countries, from 3 continents, will spend two years of their time on a joint project, it is inevitable that it is a very important discussion. And it really is. Through the activities of the project, a series of capacity building and mobility activities were realized, through which a methodology was developed that includes a framework that consists of a list of evidence-based indicators for success, enabling youth organizations to evaluate their communities, cities in order to better understand social entrepreneurial eco-systems, to better identify the area of focus and potential partners.
