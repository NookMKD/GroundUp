Quality of Life

This pillar focuses on understanding and assessing the quality of life within the ecosystem. It aims to explore how social enterprises contribute to improving the overall quality of life for individuals and the community. The indicators in this pillar reflect the factors that influence the quality of life and its relationship to social economy entities.

Human Captial

This pillar aims to understand the potential human resources within the ecosystem, including their skills, knowledge, and the users of social enterprises in the community.

Support System

This pillar focuses on the support systems in place for social enterprises, including legislation, supporting institutions, formal and informal education, networks, and culture.

Funding and access to capital

This pillar focuses on the availability of financial resources from public and private sources, both nationally and internationally, to support social enterprises.
